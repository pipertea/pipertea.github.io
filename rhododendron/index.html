Ericaceae

    Common Names: Pacific Rhododendron, California Rhododendron
    Size: can grow up to 26 feet (8 meters) if grown in shade and grows 3 to 15 feet (1 to 5 m) in height
    Where It Grows: British Columbia to California; grows west of the Cascades crest and at the coast in Washington
    Living Conditions: can tolerate sun and shade and any soil
